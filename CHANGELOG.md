- **Portal/form-only, honestly labeled**: UVU (NextRequest), DPS (GovQA with a division picker), WCSO (own web form — "DO NOT PREPAY" quirk documented), St. George (one NextRequest for Recorder+PD+Fire; the records@sgcity.org address floating around aggregator sites is on no official page — not used).
- **Medical Examiner reality check**: ME case records are restricted by Utah Code § 26B-8-217, not ordinary GRAMA — the OME card targets administrative transmittal/chain-of-custody paperwork instead and says why.
- **Statute cites verified against the code**: 10/5-day deadlines (§ 63G-2-204(4)), discretionary fee waiver (§ 63G-2-203(4)(a)), written-denial requirements (§ 63G-2-205(2)), the expected denial ground (§ 63G-2-305(10)(a)), and the appeal ladder (§ 63G-2-401/402: 30 days to the CAO, then State Records Committee or district court).
