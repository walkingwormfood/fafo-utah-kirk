## 2026-07-09 — v0: 14 findings, 21 requests, from prelim days 1–3

Launched from the preliminary-hearing record (days one through three of State v. Robinson), as surfaced by Baron Coleman (Ep. 141) and Ian Carroll's day-two stream:

- **Missing & deleted footage**: the reportedly deleted Washington County intake video (plus its retention schedule and deletion paper trail), Officer Bagley's dead bodycam and the device audit logs that would explain it.
- **Timeline contradictions**: the four irreconcilable custody times (9:00 p.m. testimony / 10:00 p.m. booking sheet / 10:26 p.m. in all 16 warrants / 4:00 a.m. formal arrest), the Twiggs interview-location flip (St. George PD vs. WCSO), the DPS plane into St. George at ~11:37 p.m.
- **Crime scene handling**: the courtyard paved over without the lead investigator's knowledge, K9 deployments with no results.
- **Physical evidence**: the .223 round on the computer science building roof and its "cycled rifle" accounting, the medical examiner chain-of-custody paperwork.
- **Video evidence**: the undisclosed edits in the courtroom compilations (the missing 1:16, out-of-order stairwell splices, the 55-minute garage gap), the Noble Nest-footage report with the bald-driver/three-passengers description.
- **Personnel & credibility**: the Brooksby severance agreement, the McBride Fifth Amendment letter.

GRAMA boilerplate throughout: § 63G-2-204 deadlines, § 63G-2-203(4) fee waiver, § 63G-2-205 written-denial demand, segregability, no-records confirmation. Follow-up drafter and custom-request drafter carried over from federal FAFO.

Every entity's submission route was audited before launch (the gotcha-hunt federal FAFO learned the hard way, done up front this time):

- **One-click email where it's real**: Utah County Sheriff (SheriffRecords@utahcounty.gov), Utah County Attorney (ucao@utahcounty.gov), Washington County Clerk/Auditor (grama@washco.utah.gov), Orem PD (records@orem.gov — the policerecords@orem.org address on older pages is a retired domain, not used), and UVU Police directly (uvpdrecords@uvu.edu, $20 minimum — a separate intake from the university's NextRequest portal, so the bodycam, scene-log, and surveillance-export requests go straight to the records custodian).
- **Portal/form-only, honestly labeled**: UVU (NextRequest), DPS (GovQA with a division picker), WCSO (own web form — "DO NOT PREPAY" quirk documented), St. George (one NextRequest for Recorder+PD+Fire; the records@sgcity.org address floating around aggregator sites is on no official page — not used).
- **Medical Examiner reality check**: ME case records are restricted by Utah Code § 26B-8-217, not ordinary GRAMA — the OME card targets administrative transmittal/chain-of-custody paperwork instead and says why.
- **Statute cites verified against the code**: 10/5-day deadlines (§ 63G-2-204(4)), discretionary fee waiver (§ 63G-2-203(4)(a)), written-denial requirements (§ 63G-2-205(2)), the expected denial ground (§ 63G-2-305(10)(a)), and the appeal ladder (§ 63G-2-401/402: 30 days to the CAO, then State Records Committee or district court).
